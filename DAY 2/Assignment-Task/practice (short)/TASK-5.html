Text formatting is an important part of writing because it helps make information clear and easy to read. By using bold text, we can highlight the most important words or ideas in a paragraph. Italic formatting is useful when we want to emphasize something gently without making it too strong. Underlining can be used to draw attention to key points or important links. Highlighting helps readers quickly notice essential information. Good formatting also makes long paragraphs look neat and well-organized. Overall, text formatting improves understanding and keeps the reader engaged.
