The Silent Garden

In the quiet garden where soft winds play,
Morning whispers gently greet the day.
Dewdrops shimmer on petals bright,
Diamonds waking in the early light.
A lone bird hums a tender tune,
Floating beneath the silver moon.
Shadows dance on emerald leaves,
Telling stories the earth once weaved.
The flowers sway with graceful art,
Nature’s pulse, a beating heart.
The sun peeks in with golden hue,
Painting dreams in shades of blue.
The world feels calm, serene, and true,
Wrapped in peace the dawn renews.
And in this place, my soul finds rest.

— By Aurora Lane
