Whispers of the Night

A Short Poem

The night sky glows with silver light,
Stars awaken, shining bright.
A gentle wind begins to roam,
Guiding lost hearts back to home.

In the quiet, dreams take flight,
Dancing softly out of sight.
Every shadow, calm and deep,
Sings a song that lulls to sleep.

Written by: Bala
Location: Chennai, India
Date: 2025
